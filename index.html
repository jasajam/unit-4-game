Crystals!!!

Instructions

  • At the start of each game, you'll see a random number. This is your goal number.
  • Click the four crystals below one-by-one to get to your goal number. Each crystal represents a different number of points, which will be added to your score when you click it, and no, you will not be shown the value of the crystal - you'll have to do math to guess ;)
  • If you meet the goal number exactly, you win!!! If you exceed the random number, you lose - sucks to your assmar.
  • After you win or lose, go ahead, play again; you'll get a new goal number and each crystal will be worth a new value of points.
